📖 Level Preview
10 Temples Await
⭐ Stage 1 — Temple Ruins
1
Hello, Temple!
Simple Intro
Each player climbs their own separate staircase on opposite sides of the temple. Simple, breezy intro to get you warmed up!
🎯 Meet your partner at the top exit ⭐
2
Push & Pull
Pressure Plates
P2 stands on the right plate to open the gate for P1. P1 climbs up and steps on the upper plate to open the gate for P2.
🎯 Both plates must be triggered in sequence
3
Lift Me Up!
Boost Jump
The high ledge is too tall for a normal jump! P2 stands in the BOOST ZONE. P1 jumps onto P2 then jumps again to rocket up.
🚀 Boost jump = jump on partner, then jump again!
4
The Vault
Keys & Doors
P1 holds the plate to open the center gate. P2 grabs the key from the right platform, walks through the gate, and unlocks the door.
🔑 Teamwork and timing is everything here
5
Grand Finale
Ultimate Challenge
STAGE 1 BOSS
P2 holds still in the BOOST ZONE so P1 can boost up to grab the key on the high shelf. P2 then moves to open the gate. P1 brings the key through. Both race to the top! 🏆
🏆 The ultimate test of coordination — don't give up!
⚠️ Stage 2 — Underground Ruins
6
Mind the Gap
Launch Pads + Holes
A wide deadly hole splits the arena. One high center platform is the only exit — too far and too high for a normal jump. Step on a LAUNCH PAD at the edge to claim it and super-jump across. Both players must land on the center platform together.
🚀 First to step on a pad claims it — coordinate who jumps when!
7
Island Hopping
Scrolling World
The world stretches wide — four islands separated by deadly chasms. Use launch pads to hop across. P2 must reach island 3 and press the plate to open P1's path. Then both climb to the exit on island 4.
🏝️ First scrolling level — the camera follows both players!
8
Bridge Builder
Ghost Bridge
A massive chasm can only be crossed via a ghost bridge — but it only appears while the plate is held. P2 must boost over a smaller gap first, reach the plate, and hold it while P1 crosses the bridge.
🌉 The bridge disappears the moment the plate is released!
9
Key Across the Void
Keys & Chasms
A key sits on an isolated island, holes on both sides. P1 holds the gate plate for P2's return. P2 leaps to the key island, grabs it, and must navigate back carrying the key to unlock the door.
🔑 Drop the key in a hole and it resets — don't fall!
10
Grand Canyon
Stage 2 Finale
STAGE 2 BOSS
Three massive chasms. Launch pads, a ghost bridge, partner boost — all in one brutal finale. Grab the key past the gate, unlock the door, and race to the very top. This is everything you've learned. 🔥
🔥 The hardest level in the game — coordination is survival!
